About Me

Hey there! I’m Sanat Ladkat—an architect turned machine learning engineer, blending the art of design with the magic of technology. With a B.Arch, M.Des, and a PGD in Data Science and AI under my belt, I’m on a mission to reshape how we interact with the world around us.

My journey started with the creative intricacies of architecture, where I learned to shape dynamic spaces that resonate with people. Then I dove into design, crafting intuitive experiences that make technology feel seamless. Now, I’m harnessing the power of Python and machine learning to build smart solutions that not only solve problems but also delight users.

I thrive at the intersection of creativity and analytics, wielding tools like Figma, Rhino, and Django to bring ideas to life. Whether it’s visualizing complex data or creating engaging interfaces, I’m all about pushing boundaries and innovating.
